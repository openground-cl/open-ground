The inquiry

A living
research initiative

Open Ground is not only a program. It is a proof-of-concept investigation into a foundational question in developmental and educational psychology.

Chronic psychological fragmentation is significantly amplified by learning environments organised around comparison, fear, and externally conditioned identity — and measurably reduced in environments organised around awareness and intrinsic understanding.
